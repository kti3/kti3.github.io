  • Kathleen is a fifth-year CS PhD student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign interested in increasing equality in the CS field. Her work focuses on cultural and pedagogical barriers to broadening participation in computing (BPC) and develops interventions to overcome them. She is advised by Colleen Lewis.
